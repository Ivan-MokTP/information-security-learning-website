What's happening? What did I just do?
This website requires a specific identity called administrator to access.

  • When you first entered random username and password, the server cannot recognize your identity as an administrator, therefore denying access.
  • On the second time you entered the correct credentials, the server recognizes your identity as an administrator, therefore granting access.
This kind of identity validation is called
"Authentication"
